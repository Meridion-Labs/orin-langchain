ORIN Employee Handbook - Chapter 3: Leave Policies

3.1 Annual Leave
All full-time employees are entitled to 20 days of annual leave per calendar year. Part-time employees receive prorated leave based on their working hours.

3.2 Sick Leave
Employees can take up to 10 days of sick leave per year. Medical certification may be required for absences exceeding 3 consecutive days.

3.3 Emergency Leave
In case of family emergencies or natural disasters, employees may take up to 5 days of emergency leave with supervisor approval.

3.4 Application Process
All leave requests must be submitted through the HR portal at least 2 weeks in advance, except for emergency situations.
